## Purpose
Define the initial steps to validate and authorize work, align stakeholders, and create a lightweight plan.

## When to use
Whenever a new project idea or feature proposal is ready to be explored.

## Goals
- Confirm business need and measurable outcome
- Identify stakeholders & champions
- Define success criteria and initial timeline
- Decide go/no-go for planning

## Minimum Deliverables
- Project One-pager (Problem, Goal, Success Metrics)
- Stakeholder list & communication plan
- High-level timeline and key milestones
- Initial risk list
- Resource needs (team roles, rough effort estimate)

## Role-Specific Responsibilities in Initiation

### Product Manager
- Leads creation of the Project One-pager with problem statement and success metrics
- Defines business value and customer impact
- Identifies primary stakeholders and champions
- Makes go/no-go recommendation based on strategic fit

### Project Manager
- Facilitates initiation meetings and stakeholder alignment
- Creates high-level timeline and identifies key milestones
- Documents initial risks and dependencies
- Coordinates resource planning and availability checks

### Business Analyst
- Supports requirements discovery and initial scope definition
- Documents stakeholder needs and constraints
- Identifies process impacts and integration requirements
- Helps validate feasibility of success metrics

### Technical Lead
- Provides technical feasibility assessment
- Identifies high-level technical risks and dependencies
- Estimates rough technical effort and complexity
- Recommends technical approach or architecture considerations

### Scrum Master / Delivery Facilitator
- Ensures team capacity is considered in planning
- Identifies process or team dependencies
- Facilitates team input on timeline and commitment level

### UX/UI Designer
- Assesses user experience implications early
- Identifies design research or discovery needs
- Flags accessibility or usability considerations upfront

### Support / Customer Success
- Provides customer feedback and pain points relevant to the initiative
- Identifies potential adoption or training needs
- Assesses support impact and readiness requirements

## Initiation Checklist
- [ ] One-pager completed and reviewed by Product Lead
- [ ] Sponsor / Stakeholder alignment (email or meeting)
- [ ] Decision: Approve to move into planning?
- [ ] Create repo or project board skeleton
- [ ] Add initial artifacts to repo (docs/ or .copilot/)

## Decision Gate
Move to planning when:
- Success metrics are clear
- Stakeholders agree on priority
- Team availability is confirmed
- Technical feasibility is validated
- Key roles are identified and committed
